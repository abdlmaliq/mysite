About Abdulmalik

After graduating from the University of Benin, Nigeria with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering in 2014, I pursued a career in IT Support while developing my web development skills.

I'm a dedicated IT Support Specialist and WordPress Designer with a passion for creating innovative and visually stunning websites. With a strong foundation in IT infrastructure and a keen eye for design, I bring a unique perspective to my work.

Since then, I have worked with top companies and well-known organizations such as Automattic, Bloomfield LP, GAIA Africa Club, Strachan Partners, FrieslandCampina Wamco.

As an IT Support Specialist, I excel at infrastructure Management, troubleshooting & problem-solving, and technical Support & Customer Service. As a WordPress Designer, I leverage my technical expertise and creative flair to Design visually appealing websites, Develop custom WordPress themes and plugins, and Optimize websites for performance and SEO.

My commitment to excellence is evident in my ability to Combine technical skills with creative vision, Stay up-to-date with industry trends, Collaborate effectively with clients.

In addition to my professional skills, I'm a dedicated individual who enjoys exploring new technologies, spending time with family and friends, and staying active. My passion for my work drives me to deliver exceptional results and exceed client expectations.
